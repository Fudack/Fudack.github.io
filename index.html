About

I’m a passionate IT engineering professional dedicated to driving innovation and sharing knowledge. With experience in managing projects and training others in technologies like home automation, process automation, and software development, I strive to empower teams and individuals. I also have skills in DevOps and hold a diploma in cybersecurity, which keeps me engaged in the ever-evolving tech landscape.

My enthusiasm for technology fuels my continuous self-learning, allowing me to stay on top of the latest trends and advancements. I’m excited to contribute innovative solutions that enhance everyday life and optimize processes through technology.

What i do

Front-end

I am a frontend developer with experience in JavaScript and TypeScript, utilizing frameworks such as React, Angular, and Ionic. I excel at creating responsive and visually appealing applications using Bootstrap and Tailwind CSS. I am committed to optimizing performance and accessibility while keeping up with the latest web development trends.

Back-end

I am a backend developer familiar with Django and Python, experienced in building robust applications. I have worked with various databases, including MySQL, MariaDB, MongoDB, Firebase (Realtime Database), and PostgreSQL. Additionally, I use R for data analysis and the creation of dashboards applying business intelligence. My focus is on building scalable and efficient solutions, ensuring seamless integration between the backend and data systems.

Consultancy

In addition to my development services, I offer strategic consulting to help businesses define effective roadmaps. I specialize in digital transformation for traditional organizations that have yet to migrate. My services include business intelligence, data analysis, cybersecurity consulting, and penetration testing for organizations. I am committed to guiding businesses toward innovation and security in the digital landscape.
